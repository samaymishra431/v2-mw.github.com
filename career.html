BUILDING A PLATFORM FOR COMMUNITY, NOT BUSINESS

BOI Matrimony is not a corporate company — it is a seva platform run by the Brahmin Organisation of India (BOI). We do not earn profits. We exist only to serve Brahmin families by providing genuine, safe, and value-driven matrimonial facilities. There is no monthly or yearly fee. Only a one-time seva-dakshina of ₹2100 ensures lifetime partnership. This contribution supports community welfare, sustains volunteers & workers, and maintains the facilities we provide.

Our Mission

To unite Brahmin families with love, respect, and dignity — preserving culture and serving society without profit motive.

Our Vision

A strong, united Brahmin community where traditions thrive, and marriages are built on values, not commercial gain.

Our History

BOI Matrimony was created as a seva-driven alternative to profit-based portals, helping thousands of families unite.

Our Values

Seva, Sanskriti, Satya, Samarpan & Sahabhagita — the guiding principles that keep this platform pure and community-focused.
